Pay It Forward

Acts of Kindness Impact Our Community

The concept is simple. In this world of uncertainty and economic hardship, How can an ordinary person, …You or Me, make a positive difference?

Although the concept might seem simple... the outcome of your gift, will drastically change our community.

Today, there are so many people in our community who are in need. So, you do something to help. Call us with your slightly used or unused Medical Equipment, Medical Supplies, Mobility or Incontinence Products. Affordable Medical will pick up your donation, clean and sanitize the equipment, and call a local non-profit for them to gift ones of their families in need. We then, deliver and set it up in their family’s home. That is how one person (you), by one person (me) at a time, can make a huge change in our community.
