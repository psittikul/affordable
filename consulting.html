Consulting and Services

Affordable Medical provides a wide range of services that improve, affect and enhance the quality of life.

Affordable Medical's Staff has a wealth of experience in providing specialized care for our clients. You can schedule a personal consulting time with June McCarthy. She will sit with you and go over any problems, care concerns, or frustrations regarding how you can better care for your loved one. After our personal meeting with you, our team then meets for discussions, then researches, plans and provides a proposal that fits your financial, personal, and healthcare needs. We are select, discreet, unfailingly earnest in our efforts, and dedicated to our mission statement. We pride ourselves in being efficient and highly capable with impeccable manners. We understand that care giving is very rewarding, however, the pressure of balancing work, family, and play is the primary stress factor in care giving today. We recognize the difficulty and hardship that this places upon you.

Let our 39 years experience help you provide options to solve your needs.
