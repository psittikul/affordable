About Us

Affordable Medical Supply is an accredited premier healthcare provider of medical equipment, healthcare supplies, personal care products, rentals and repairs for over 39 years.

We recognize that one single clients needs, are never the same as another. Through careful assessments with the client, the family or care managers, we focus on selecting the most appropriate equipment ,supplies or personal care products that will provide the positive outcomes.

We specialize in care giving solutions when standard solutions won’t solve the issues at hand.

Affordable Medical Supply has been a staple in Broward County for almost 40 years, however, our services also encompass the Palm Beaches area, and shipping throughout the U.S.A.. Our large retail location is a place where families and healthcare professionals can visit and choose their equipment , or sit privately with a consultant to discuss the needs and recommendations for your review.

Affordable is a Certified Repair Center for all types of equipment. Accredited by HQAA. O & P Assistant Fitters. Respiratory and REHAB Equipment for long term care needs. Personal Care Products for all Ages.
