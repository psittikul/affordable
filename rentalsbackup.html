Private Rental Equipment for

  • Families
  • Travelers
  • For Discharges

If you have a family member who is Traveling, or in need of medical equipment but does not immediately qualify through your specific insurance, you can get equipment quickly by privately renting from Affordable Medical Supply.

Delivery, Set Up and Equipment education are all part of our standard care.

If you cannot get a verification of benefits from your insurance company in time for your need, you can privately rent it from us until you get everything certified. We provide rental equipment to facilities that need additional equipment to provide care, but do not want to buy it permanently. Our facility coordinator can provide a rental program for the rentals of specialized equipment such as Low Air Loss Mattresses, Patient Lifts and Rehab Equipment. We deliver, set- up and maintain them all on an As Need Basis.

Let us know if you are a snowbird and only here for a few months…we have the most fair and honest rates available in town.

All rentals require:

Credit Card, Drivers License, the Rental Agreement Signed, and the 1st months rental cost.

Doing Private Rentals for all types of entities such as:

Private Individuals, Traveling Patients, Independent and Assisted Living Communities, Rehab Facilities, Non Profit Organizations and Healthcare Teaching Locations.

Partial List of Equipment:

Hospital Beds Air Loss Mattresses Oxygen & Concentrators Nebulizers Wheelchairs Walkers Lift Chairs Power Wheelchairs Scooters Patient Lifts I.V.Poles Oxygen tanks Mattresses
